A SONDHEIM CELEBRATION WITH KEVINSTITES

"A Sondheim Celebration with Kevin Stites" is a musical tribute to the legendary composer Stephen Sondheim, featuring some of his most beloved songs from shows such as "Sweeney Todd," "Into the Woods," "Company," and more. The show features a talented cast of singers and dancers, accompanied by a full orchestra conducted by Kevin Stites. The performances showcase Sondheim's brilliant lyrics and complex melodies, while also exploring the themes of love, loss, and the human experience that are at the heart of his work. The show is a must-see for any fan of musical theater and Sondheim's contributions to the art form.

Hollywood Bowl , On December 30 , 2023

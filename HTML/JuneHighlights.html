THE LONELY FEW

Lila (Lauren Patten, Tony Award winner for Jagged Little Pill) is getting by in her Kentucky hometown—scanning groceries at the Save-A-Lot, caring for her erratic brother, and living for Friday nights, when she plays a gig with her band The Lonely Few. And that's enough. Or she thought it was, until Amy (Ciara Renée, Frozen, Waitress), an established musician \ragged from the road, passes through and offers her a shot at something much, much bigger. But is Lila ready for the life she never dared to imagine? A world premiere musical by Zoe Sarnak and Rachel Bonds, The Lonely Few is a love story between two women searching for a sense of home.A world premiere musical by Zoe Sarnak and Rachel Bonds, The Lonely Few is a love story between two women searching for a sense of home. A LITTLE NIGHT MUSIC

"A Little Night Music" is a Tony Award-winning musical that follows the romantic entanglements of several couples during a weekend in the country. The plot centers around a renowned actress named Desiree Armfeldt, who reconnects with an old flame named Fredrik Egerman, who is currently married to a much younger woman named Anne. The other characters in the story include the jealous Count Carl-Magnus, his long-suffering wife Charlotte, and their servant Petra. The musical is known for its intricate score by Stephen Sondheim, including the popular songs "Send in the Clowns" and "A Weekend in the Country," as well as its clever lyrics and witty dialogue. Overall, "A Little Night Music" is a sophisticated and charming musical that explores the complexities of love and desire with humor and insight.

Pasadena Playhouse, From June 25 - July 21, 2023

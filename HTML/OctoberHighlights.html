INTO THE WOODS

"Into the Woods" is a musical that intertwines the plots of several classic fairy tales, including Cinderella, Jack and the Beanstalk, Little Red Riding Hood, and Rapunzel. The story follows a baker and his wife who are trying to break a curse placed on their family by a witch. They journey into the woods to find the ingredients needed for the curse-breaking spell and cross paths with other characters from the fairy tales along the way. The musical explores themes of family, love, loss, and the consequences of our actions. The show features Stephen Sondheim's signature witty lyrics and intricate melodies, as well as stunning visuals and powerful performances.

Ahmanson Theatre , From September 27 - October 30 , 2023

MORGAM WALLEN

Morgan Wallen is a country music singer and songwriter from Tennessee. He gained fame in 2018 with his debut album "If I Know Me," which features hit singles like "Up Down" and "Whiskey Glasses." In 2021, he released his second album "Dangerous: The Double Album," which became a massive commercial success, reaching number one on the Billboard 200 chart. Despite his success, Wallen has also faced controversy due to his use of racial slurs and other inappropriate behavior. Nonetheless, his music has continued to resonate with fans of country music.

SoFi Stadium , On October 22 , 2023

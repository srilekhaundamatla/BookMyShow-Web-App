ANASTASIA

Anastasia is a spectacular musical that tells the story of a young woman named Anya, who may or may not be the long-lost princess Anastasia Romanov. Set in the midst of the Russian Revolution, the musical takes us on a journey through the streets of St. Petersburg, Paris, and beyond, as Anya sets out to discover the truth about her past and find her rightful place in the world. With breathtaking sets and costumes, soaring musical numbers, and a cast of unforgettable characters, Anastasia is a must-see musical that will leave you spellbound from beginning to end. It's a story of hope, love, and resilience, that reminds us of the power of family and the importance of never giving up on our dreams.

Fred Kavil Theatre, From May 27 - 30, 2023

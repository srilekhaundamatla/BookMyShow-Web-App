WHY WATCH AVA - THE SECRET CONVERSATIONS ?

ONCE UPON A TIME

"Ava: The Secret Conversations." offers a fascinating behind-the-scenes look at the life of one of the most iconic actresses of the era. Additionally, the book provides insights into the inner workings of the Hollywood studio system and the personal lives of many of the industry's biggest stars. The conversations between Gardner and Evans are also highly personal and revealing, discussing topics such as love, sex, marriage, and the struggles of fame and success. As such, the book provides a window into the personal and emotional lives of two highly talented and complex individuals.

WHAT'S THE STORY?

Ava: The Secret Conversations" is a book that contains conversations between Ava Gardner, the Hollywood actress, and Peter Evans, a British journalist who interviewed her in the early 1980s. The book offers a unique insight into Gardner's life, career, and personal relationships, and provides a glimpse into the golden age of Hollywood.

BULLETS ON BROADWAY

"Bullets Over Broadway" that was written by Woody Allen and based on his 1994 film of the same name. The musical follows a struggling playwright named David Shayne who receives financial backing from a gangster in exchange for casting his talentless girlfriend in the lead role. The plot is set against the backdrop of the Roaring Twenties and features lively song and dance numbers. The musical was nominated for six Tony Awards and is known for its colorful costumes, intricate choreography, and nostalgic atmosphere. Overall, "Bullets Over Broadway" is a fun and entertaining musical that captures the spirit of a bygone era.

SIX

"Six" is a musical that retells the story of Henry VIII's six wives from their own perspective, through modern pop music and choreography. Each of the queens performs a solo number, showcasing their unique personalities and experiences. The musical is fast-paced, energetic, and features strong vocal performances from the cast. It also incorporates themes of female empowerment and solidarity. "Six" has been praised for its catchy songs, clever lyrics, and clever staging. Overall, "Six" is a fun and fresh take on a historical story, with a modern twist that appeals to audiences of all ages.

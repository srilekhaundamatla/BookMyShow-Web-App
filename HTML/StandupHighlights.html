BILL BURR

Bill Burr is a stand-up comedian known for his unique style of comedy that often touches on taboo topics such as race, gender, and politics. He delivers his material with a sharp wit and biting sarcasm that has won him a large and dedicated following. Burr has released several successful comedy specials, including "I'm Sorry You Feel That Way" and "Paper Tiger," and has also acted in various films and television shows. He continues to tour extensively, performing to sold-out crowds around the world.

GABRIEL IGLESIAS

Gabriel Iglesias, also known as "Fluffy," is a Mexican-American comedian known for his stand-up comedy and voice acting roles. He is particularly known for his jokes about his weight and his use of sound effects and storytelling in his performances. Iglesias has released several comedy specials, including "I'm Not Fat...I'm Fluffy" and "One Show Fits All," and has appeared in numerous TV shows and films, including "Magic Mike," "Coco," and "The Nut Job." His humor often centers around his experiences growing up as a Mexican-American in California, and he is beloved by many for his relatable and engaging performances.

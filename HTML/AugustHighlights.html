CATS

"Cats" is a musical that follows the story of a group of Jellicle cats, who gather for their annual Jellicle Ball. Throughout the show, each cat introduces themselves and shares their unique story and personality through song and dance. The main focus is on Grizabella, an older cat who is ostracized by the others, and her journey of redemption. The show features elaborate costumes and makeup, as well as memorable songs like "Memory." "Cats" has been praised for its spectacle and energy, as well as its emotional depth and exploration of themes such as identity and acceptance. Despite some mixed reviews, "Cats" has remained a popular and beloved musical for over three decades.

Fred Kavil Theatre, From August 11 - 14 , 2023

HAIRSPRAY

"Hairspray" is a musical comedy set in 1962 Baltimore that tells the story of Tracy Turnblad, a plus-sized teenager with a big heart and even bigger dreams. Despite being ridiculed by her peers, Tracy is determined to dance on "The Corny Collins Show," a local TV dance program. With the help of her best friend Penny, her supportive parents, and the handsome Link Larkin, Tracy sets out to integrate the show and promote racial harmony. However, she faces opposition from the show's former star, Amber Von Tussle, and her mother, the racist Velma. With infectious music, upbeat dance numbers, and a heartwarming message, "Hairspray" celebrates diversity, acceptance, and the power of following your dreams.

Dolby Theatre , From August 2 - 21 , 2023

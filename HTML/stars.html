WHY WATCH STARS ON ICE ?

ONCE UPON A TIME

One of the unique features of Stars on Ice is the level of creativity and innovation that goes into each performance. Skaters are encouraged to push the boundaries of what is possible in figure skating, often incorporating complex lifts, jumps, and spins into their routines.In addition to being an entertaining spectacle, Stars on Ice is also a showcase of the incredible athleticism and artistry of figure skating. It provides an opportunity for audiences to see some of the world's best skaters up close and in person, and to witness the beauty and excitement of this elegant sport.

WHAT'S THE STORY?

Stars on Ice is a touring figure skating show that features a variety of professional skaters performing choreographed routines to music. The show was founded in the United States in 1986 and has since become a popular entertainment option for skating enthusiasts and fans of the performing arts. The skaters who participate in Stars on Ice are typically top-level competitive skaters who have retired from competition and turned professional. The show often features Olympic and World champions, and the skaters are known for their technical skill, artistry, and showmanship.

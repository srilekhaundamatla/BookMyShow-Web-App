THUNDER FROM DOWN UNDER

Thunder From Down Under is a male revue show featuring a cast of Australian dancers who perform various dance routines and stripteases in a Las Vegas-style setting. The show is known for its energetic and interactive performances, with audience members often being invited on stage for various segments. The dancers showcase their impressive physiques and athletic abilities while entertaining the crowd with their charismatic personalities and cheeky humor. Overall, Thunder From Down Under is a fun and exciting show that promises a memorable night out for fans of male revue and entertainment.

STARS ON ICE

"Stars on Ice" is a touring ice show that features some of the world's most talented figure skaters performing breathtaking routines on ice. The show was created by Olympic gold medalist Scott Hamilton and has featured many other Olympic and world champion skaters throughout the years. The performances are set to a variety of music and include both solo and group routines, as well as some comedic moments. The show is known for its high-energy, stunning choreography and amazing athleticism, making it a must- see for any fan of figure skating or live performance.

LION KING

The Lion King tells the story of Simba (Swahili for lion), a lion cub who is to succeed his father, Mufasa, as King of the Pride Lands; however, after his paternal uncle Scar kills Mufasa to seize the throne, Simba is tricked into believing he was responsible for his father's death and flees into exile. After growing up in the company of the carefree outcasts Timon and Pumbaa, Simba receives valuable perspective from his childhood friend, Nala, and his shaman, Rafiki, before returning to challenge Scar to end his tyranny and take his place in the Circle of Life as the rightful king.

Pantages Theater Hollywood, Until Mar 26, 2023

LES MISERABLES

"Les Misérables" is a musical based on Victor Hugo's novel of the same name. Set in early 19th-century France, it follows the story of Jean Valjean, a former prisoner who seeks redemption while being pursued by the ruthless police inspector Javert. The show features iconic songs like "I Dreamed a Dream," "On My Own," and "Do You Hear the People Sing?" as it explores themes of love, sacrifice, and revolution. With its epic scale and emotional depth, "Les Misérables" has become one of the most beloved and enduring musicals of all time.

Pantages Theatre Hollywood, From August 1 - September 10, 2023

SIX

"Six" is a musical that retells the story of Henry VIII's six wives from their own perspective, through modern pop music and choreography. Each of the queens performs a solo number, showcasing their unique personalities and experiences. The musical is fast-paced, energetic, and features strong vocal performances from the cast. It also incorporates themes of female empowerment and solidarity. "Six" has been praised for its catchy songs, clever lyrics, and clever staging. Overall, "Six" is a fun and fresh take on a historical story, with a modern twist that appeals to audiences of all ages.

Pantages Theatre Hollywood, From July 9 - August 10, 2023

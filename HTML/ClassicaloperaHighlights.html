ANDREA BOCELLI

Andrea Bocelli is an Italian opera singer, songwriter, and record producer who has become one of the most popular and beloved vocalists of all time. Blind since childhood, Bocelli began his career in the early 1990s, initially gaining attention for his stunning performances of opera classics. Since then, he has gone on to record and perform a wide range of musical styles, including pop, rock, and traditional Italian songs. Bocelli's incredible vocal range, soulful delivery, and powerful stage presence have earned him countless fans around the world, and he continues to be one of the most sought-after performers in the music industry today.

LOS ANGELES OPERA - OTELLO

"Otello" is an Italian opera in four acts composed by Giuseppe Verdi based on the Shakespearean tragedy "Othello." The story follows the moorish general Otello, who becomes consumed by jealousy when he believes his wife Desdemona has been unfaithful to him. The Los Angeles Opera's production of "Otello" features the acclaimed conductor James Conlon, and a talented cast of singers, including Russell Thomas in the title role, as well as Guanqun Yu as Desdemona and George Gagnidze as Iago. With its powerful story, exquisite music, and dynamic performances, "Otello" is a stunning production that is sure to captivate opera lovers and newcomers alike.

Dorothy Chandler Pavilion , From May 9 - June 4 , 2023

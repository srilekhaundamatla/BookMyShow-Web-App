BEETLEJUICE

"Beetlejuice" is a musical based on the 1988 film of the same name. The story follows the recently deceased couple, Barbara and Adam, who are stuck haunting their old house in New England. When a new family moves in, the couple tries to scare them off, but their efforts are thwarted by the mischievous and chaotic ghost, Beetlejuice. As the story unfolds, Beetlejuice tricks the family into letting him take over their lives, but his actions have unintended consequences that threaten the very existence of the living and the dead. The musical features an upbeat and energetic score with a mix of rock and pop music, as well as spectacular visual effects and choreography that bring the world of the afterlife to life on stage.

Pantages Theatre Hollywood , From November 11 - 30 , 2023

ANTZ

"Antz" is a computer-animated adventure-comedy film released in 1998. The story follows Z, an ant with individualistic tendencies, who yearns for a life beyond his mundane existence. Z's journey begins when he falls in love with the beautiful Princess Bala and decides to switch places with his soldier ant friend, Weaver, to attend a parade where he can see her. However, things go awry when Z inadvertently becomes embroiled in a plot by the power-hungry General Mandible to launch a war against a neighboring ant colony. The movie showcases Z's efforts to stop the impending conflict and find his place in the world while also addressing themes of individuality, conformity, and social hierarchy. The film features a star-studded voice cast, including Woody Allen, Sharon Stone, Sylvester Stallone, Jennifer Lopez, and Gene Hackman.

Audrey Skirball Kenis Theater , From November 20 - December 30, 2023

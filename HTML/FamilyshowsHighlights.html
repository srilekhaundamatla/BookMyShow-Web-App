JURASSIC WORLD LIVE

Jurassic World Live is a thrilling live-action show that takes audiences on an epic adventure to Isla Nublar, the home of Jurassic World. The story follows a team of scientists who are trying to protect the dinosaurs on the island from a dangerous new threat. Along the way, they encounter a host of incredible creatures, including velociraptors, triceratops, and of course, the mighty T-Rex. With breathtaking stunts, stunning special effects, and a heart-pumping soundtrack, Jurassic World Live is an unforgettable experience for fans of all ages.

Honda Center Anaheim , From July 14 - 16 , 2023

MONSTER JAM

Monster Jam is a live motorsport event featuring some of the world's most iconic monster trucks, including Grave Digger, Max-D, and El Toro Loco. The event consists of various competitions, including racing and freestyle, where drivers compete for the highest scores by performing impressive stunts and maneuvers such as high jumps, backflips, and donuts. With exciting pyrotechnics, electrifying music, and high-energy performances, Monster Jam is a thrilling and unforgettable experience for fans of all ages who are looking for adrenaline-pumping entertainment.

Angel Stadium Of Anaheim , On November 11 , 2023

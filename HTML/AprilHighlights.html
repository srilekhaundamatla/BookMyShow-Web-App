RUSSIAN GRAND BALLET: SWAN LAKE

Russian Grand Ballet's production of "Swan Lake" is a stunning showcase of classical ballet at its finest. Featuring world-class dancers and exquisite choreography, this timeless tale of love and betrayal comes to life on stage in a dazzling display of beauty and grace. From the sweeping, romantic movements of the corps de ballet to the powerful and emotional solos of the lead dancers, every moment of this performance is breathtaking. The lavish sets and costumes transport the audience to another time and place, while Tchaikovsky's timeless score provides the perfect backdrop for this timeless story of passion and redemption. Whether you are a lifelong ballet enthusiast or simply looking for an unforgettable night out, Russian Grand Ballet's "Swan Lake" is a must-see event that is sure to leave you spellbound.

Irvine Barclay Theatre, From April 18 - 19, 2023

SUNDAY IN THE PARK WITH GEORGE

"Sunday in the Park with George" is a powerful musical that explores the relationship between art, creativity, and human connection. The story follows George Seurat, a passionate painter who struggles to balance his artistic vision with the demands of his personal life. As he creates his masterpiece, "A Sunday Afternoon on the Island of La Grande Jatte," he grapples with love, loss, and the sacrifices that must be made in the pursuit of greatness. Through a series of hauntingly beautiful songs and poignant scenes, this musical explores the complexities of the human heart and the enduring power of art to connect us all. With a cast of talented performers, stunning visuals, and a soaring score, "Sunday in the Park with George" is an unforgettable theatrical experience that will leave you moved, inspired, and profoundly transformed.

Pasadena Playhouse, Until April 19, 2023
